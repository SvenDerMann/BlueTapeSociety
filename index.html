Welcome to Blue Tape Society


Events Contact

Blue Tape Society is a group for artists to help their art get exposure. Blue Tape hosts art exhibitions as a means for young artists to do so, while also serving as a platform to create networks between like-minded artists.

Blue Tape was founded by a Pace University student who aimed to help her peers create a foundation for their artist careers.
